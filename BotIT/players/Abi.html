Asset Rollup

Depression

  • Roll a d12 at the start of every Scene. On a 10+, Character is Depressed (Lost Love, poor)

Combat

  • Enemies can declare as Agressors in the first round of Combat, but must pay an activation cost equal to half the Characters Daring score - in this case, three points (Dreaded, good)
  • Offensive Maneuvers against you cost an extra point to execute (Dreaded, good)
  • Fighting in Bright Light reduces the Melee Pool by one or two dice, depending on brightness (Night-sighted, poor)
  • Fighting in Bright Light reduces the Archery Pool by two or four dice, depending on brightness (Night-sighted, poor)

Activities in Bright Light

  • +1 penalty to all activities

Social Interactions

  • +1 bonus for friendly interactions with sexually compatible people due to attraction (Legendary Beauty, good)
  • +1 bonus to Intimidation Checks (Dreaded, good)
  • +2 penalty for friendly interactions with sexually incompatible people due to jealousy (Legendary Beauty, good)
  • +2 penalty when dealing with Hillmen, Nomads, and Savages due to creepiness (Inhuman, poor)
  • +1 penalty when dealing with Civilized people due to creepiness (Inhuman, poor)
    • If Depressed, all friendly interactions take a further +2 penalty (Lost Love, poor)

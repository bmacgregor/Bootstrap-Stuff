Beaks

The wicked beak of birds of prey.

Reach Attack Target Number Damage Rating Blunt Damage Rating
Hand 5 -2 -4

Beak damage is Cleaving.

Cannot be used with the Raking Maneuver.

The final Wound Level inflicted cannot be higher than the attacker’s Brawn.

Claws

Medium claws are those found on the vast majority of mammals and reptiles. Large claws are those that seem oversized even in relation to their owner; hardly any real animal would qualify for this.

Weapon Reach Attack Target Number Damage Rating
Medium Claws Hand 5 -1
Large Claws Hand 5 -1

Claw damage is Blunt.

Medium claws inflict +1 Blood loss on any wound of Level 3 and above. This does not apply against any type of metal armor.

Large claws inflict +1 Blood loss on any wound. This does not apply against any type of metal armor.

Horns

The horns of many types of animal. Piercing should only be used for the most acutely pointed types of horns; most cattle certainly don’t qualify, for example.

Reach Attack Target Number Blunt Damage Rating Pierce Damage Rating
Hand 6 -1 -1

Horn damage is Blunt or Piercing, depending on the horn.

Butts with horns are delivered with the Bash Maneuver (see description of animal version of this Maneuver).

Horn attacks inflicting Blunt damage cause +1 Shock.

Horn attacks causing Piercing damage have –1 Damage Rating against armor.

Kicks

The kicks of most types of large hooved animals, usually delivered with much force but little skill.

Weapon Reach Attack Target Number Damage Rating
Front Kick Hand 6 -2
Back Kick Hand, Short 6 -1

Kick damage is Blunt.

Back kicks can be delivered equally at Hand and Short Reach.

Kicks carry an Activation Cost of 2 MP and attack with the Bash Maneuver (see description of animal version of this Maneuver).

Front kicks are rearing attacks with the fore legs striking usually from above and using Swing Target Zones, back kicks are attacks of, usually both hind legs lashing out to the back and use Thrust Target Zones.

Front kicks cause +1 Shock.

Back kicks cause +2 Shock and +1 Knockdown (minimum 1).

Talons

The talons of birds of prey, used with the Raking Maneuver.

Reach Attack Target Number Damage Rating Blunt Damage Rating
Hand 5 -3 -5

Talon Damage is Cleaving.

The final Wound Level inflicted cannot be higher than half the attacker’s Brawn.

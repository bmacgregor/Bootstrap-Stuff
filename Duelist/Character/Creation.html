Attributes

While a Character's Arc determines how they are role-played, attributes define what they're capable of, in-game. Every Character begins play with thirty points to spend on attributes, with the sole caution that only one attribute can be as low as 1, and only one can be as high as 8. Beyond this, Players can spend their points any way they like. Bonuses to each attribute are listed below.

Intellect

Intellect affects the strength of a Character's spells, with more intelligent Characters able to create powerful effects. Genius-level Mages are able to strip the flesh from their opponent's bones and warp reality around themselves with ease.(Spell power bonus)

Brawn

Brawn modifies the amount of raw, physical damage a Duelist is capable of dishing out, with the mightiest of heroes able to cripple an opponent with a single strike. (Physical damage bonus)

Cunning

(Bonuses to casting time and expanding the scope of a spell)

Agility

(Bonuses to evasion, speed, and dealing with multiple opponents)

Will

(Bonuses to spell charging, maintaining spells, and mental defences)

Endurance

(Bonuses to stamina, health, and encumbrance)
